## About Me

Hi, I am Yuanming Shao. I am a second-year master's student at the University of Pennsylvania, in the Computer and Information Science (CIS) Department, where I am fortunate to work with [Ryan Marcus](https://rmarcus.info/blog/) and [Zack Ives](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~zives/). Before this, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech University.

I am broadly interested in efficient and interpretable machine learning, deep generative models, machine learning systems, and the design of agentic harnesses.
